Some Good Movies According to Fred

A few of my top movies of all time...

Interstellar

I love anything to do with space, interstellar travel and black holes. Bonus as it was based on real science (though they did take some liberties!).

Inception

Another Christopher Nolan classic. The visuals were jaw dropping for its time. Great cast, great story, and just great entertainment.

Dune

One of my favourite books in the hands of one of my favourite directors, Denis Villenueve. Breathtaking visuals and story telling!
